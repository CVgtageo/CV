Music

I experiment with a few styles of electronic music and I have been using fruityloops since ~2012.

You could actually say that Ive created more music sketches than visual art sketches(and Ive done quite a bit of sketching). I am inspired by things that are not audio and I often try to mix in popular percussion styles with what might be considered 80s melodies. Ive been told by some people, that it sounds like something you would hear at the end of a movie. I also started writing edgy and even offensive fictional lyrics a few years ago that are a part of a horror film antagonist psychopath-like persona that preys on people who actively embrace the products of exploitative corporations and cultural flagships that encourage the perespective of difference by nation and race without ever being privelaged enough to understand te complexioty of world history and international relations (especially in the last 100 years), or the source of food(sometimes with my self, included). Sometimes I will take samples from a song from 70s to 80s , cut up parts of it, remix it and write over it. Some old melodies can be really inspiring to write over, and in my opinion, especially from a time of electronic disco funk that circulated from late 70s to late 80s. This is not something that I would generally show an employer but I think that the right person might appreciate this. recently I started imparting my electric guitar playing however I havent practiced for many years since my wrist had become chronically fractured.
